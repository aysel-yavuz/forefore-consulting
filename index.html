How does it work?

Think of Forefront Consulting as the Concierge of IT Advisory.

Privacy. As soon as you reach out to us, we can start working on a Non-Disclosure agreement, so that you can feel comfortable explaining your project to us. The Forefront Consulting Core-Team will learn about your project and check among our network of specialists who would be ideal to serve you.

Once we identify the best industry leaders that could help you achieve your goals, a proposal will be presented to you, including the prices and the name of the professionals that would be part of your Advisory Board. The proposal usually involves blocks of twenty hours of consulting services, which you could renew if you feel necessary.

If you do accept the proposal, the member (or members) selected for your Advisory Board will also be requested to sign the Non-Disclosure agreement. We will debrief the professors and lead experts regarding some of the most elementary information of your project to help situate them.

And then the work starts! We will gather everyone on online meetings to better understand the challenges that you are facing, whether an exisiting project or the preparation for a coming project.

The Advisory Board will investigate, possibly keeping in contact with you and your team to ask more questions. At some point, the Advisory Board will present to you their findings and deliver a report that you can use to guide your project in the right direction. This cycle of questions, investigation, and reporting, can continue for as long that necessary.

Case Study

  • Global Financial company needed to find a PDF viewer with redacting capabilities. They were about o purchase one based on nothing more than influence from advertising. However, one of our advisors performed an thorough research, lisiting all products in the market and taking notes of all of their resources, including prices, documention, and features that the company would need in a near future... and the solution was different from the one that the company initially considered. The company was able to get ahead of the competitors with a solution that was (surprisingly) a perfect fit for their industry and also reduce costs by not needing to acquire new products for the following projects that came down the line.
  • A fin-tech company needed to create a robust, lean and performant UI Component Library but wasn't sure on how to make it on a way that could cater to their multiple lines of business. One of our advisors, very experienced on that, successfully guided the creation of their development enviroment and workflow, putting in place the needed structure for testing and self-auto-documentation, leading to a code that has been easy to maintain and extend. It revolutionized the company design system, making it possible to stablish a strong visual identity across all lines of businesses and reducing development time in around 30%.
  • One of our advisors was able to create three plans on how to implement the concept of a "Smart Broom" to be used on the cleaning of a restaurant chain. It was a huge success, because besides being easy to implement it was also low cost, proving that not all inovation require high investments.

FAQ

  1. What affect the price?

    The price might change depending on the advisor that gets selected to your project. Also, if your project requires a multi-disciplinary group, having multiple advisor would increase the investment necessary. However, the Forefront's Core-Team is highly experienced and usually performe a lot of the basic leg-work, so that when the Professors and lead experts arrive, their time can be better used, and consequently lowering the costs.

  2. Do I get to choose the advisors?

    We do our best to make that happen. However, some of our advisors cannot assist certain industries because of their non-compete clauses with their main employers. Also, it could happen that the advisor already is completely booked with other projects. However, our large network of contacts within the Academia and the industry, allow us to have many professionals that are some of the best in their field.

  3. Can I request consulting for just a couple of hours?

    Yes you can. However, the prices go significantly down the more hours you purchase.

  4. Do I need to involve my team during the consulting?

    No. We understand the privacy is important and that sometimes you just want a personal consultation with our experts. Therefore, you can rest assured that our communication will remain private.

  5. Do we need to sign an official non-disclosure agreement?

    No. That is up to you, of course. Some people prefer to create hypotetical cases, generalizing the actual issue their companies are facing, so that we do not need to officially involve their employers.
